Ian's Armoury


Vehicles from WW1

Renault FT

The Renault FT was one of the first tanks in the world, and the only at the time which had a rotary turret, a huge innovation.
Sopwith Camel

The Sopwith Camel was one of the first planes to have clockwork synchronized guns that shoot through its propeller.

Vehicles from WW2


F4U Corsair

The Corsair was very important because it was one of the best attack aircraft during the warm because of its high load capability.
M4 Sherman

The M4 Sherman was an all round good tank which became the basis for many modifications and variants.
PT Boat

The PT Boat was used to support beach landings and assaults. They were heavily armed with anti air guns, and as such were a very good all round boat.

Vehicles from The Korean War


F-86 Sabre

The F-86 was the first swept wing jet, able to catch up with the Korean MiG-15, becoming an important innovation in the history of aviation.
M46 Patton

An iconic American main battle tank, the M46 Pattons was designed to replace old M26 Pershing tanks and M4 Shermans.

Vehicles from The Vietnam War


A-26 Invader

The A-26 invader was used extensively during the vietnam war for close air support and bombing supply trails.
M48 Patton

The M48 Patton was a tank used to help support the foundation of independent south vietnamese tank corps and combined forces.
Patrol Boat, River

The Patrol, or PBR was a patrol boat designed to be a fast, hard hitting scout boat, deployed extensively.
